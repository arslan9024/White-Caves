### **HUMAN-AI COLLABORATION PROTOCOLS**

**Rule 1: Clear Decision Boundaries**
- **AI DOES:** Data analysis, routine task automation, pattern recognition, initial customer triage
- **HUMANS DO:** Strategic decisions, client negotiations, complex problem-solving, personnel management

**Rule 2: Escalation Protocols**
- All AI assistants have predefined thresholds for escalating to human counterparts
- Example: AI Assistant Laila flags transactions >$50,000 for human review

**Rule 3: Training & Supervision**
- Human department heads train AI assistants on company-specific processes
- Weekly review sessions where humans validate AI performance and adjust parameters

**Rule 4: Accountability Framework**
- Humans are 100% accountable for decisions, even when based on AI recommendations
- All AI actions are logged with responsible human overseer identified
