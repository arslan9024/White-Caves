**Specifications for the New Sidebar (`AssistantNavSidebar.jsx`):**

2.  **Visual Hierarchy:**

4.  **Functionality Preservation:** Clicking an assistant must trigger the **exact same Redux action/state update** that currently loads that assistant's dashboard. No logic changes.
