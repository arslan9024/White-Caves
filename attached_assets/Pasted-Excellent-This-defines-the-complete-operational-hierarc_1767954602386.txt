### üîÑ **Integrated Workflow Example: From Lead to Prize**
1.  **Lead Capture:** **Olivia** generates a lead from Instagram, creating a `new_lead` event.
2.  **Command Center Analysis:** **Zoe** analyzes the lead quality and recommends it to the Operations dashboard.
3.  **Task Creation & Assignment:** An Operations Manager uses **Aurora's dashboard** to create a "Qualify Lead" task and assigns it to **Agent Sara**.
4.  **Task Execution:** Sara contacts the client via **Linda**. She qualifies them, books a viewing, and eventually closes a sale.
5.  **Payment Integration:** At the "collect deposit" stage, Sara clicks **"Request Payment"** in the task. **Theodora** generates an invoice, and upon payment, updates the task status.
6.  **Immutable Logging:** Every step (assignment, client call, viewing scheduled, payment received) is emitted as an event and recorded by **Henry**.
7.  **Performance Calculation:** At month's end, the Sales Department Head's dashboard queries **Henry** for all events related to Sara, runs the scoring formula, and identifies her as the top performer.
8.  **Prize Awarded:** The system flags Sara as the winner, automating the prize notification and process.
